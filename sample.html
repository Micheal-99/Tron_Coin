What is TRONLIVE?

TRONLIVE is a shared capital distribution platform, managed automatically, securely and transparently through a verified smart contract within the Tron Blockchain. No one can ever modify it or make it disappear.

How much I can earn here?

You can earn maximum 200% of your active deposit.

What is the minimum and maximum investment amount?

Minimum to start is 100 TRX and maximum has no limit.

Is TRONLIVE a secure platform?

Yes! TRONLIVE is a 100% safe and secure platform as it is developed on Tron Blockchain. And no one can hack Blockchain.

How much is referral commission?

You can earn 10% referral commission, which is divided in to 5 levels. Direct referral or level-1 commission is 5%. Level-2 commission is 2%, Level-3 ,Level-4 and Level-5 is 1% each.

What is ACTIVE DEPOSIT?

The invested amount is your active deposit. Suppose you have invested 1000 TRX then your active deposit is 1000 TRX.

How to deposit or invest in TRONLIVE?

To deposit you can use TRON LINK PRO wallet. Download TRON LINK PRO wallet in your mobile phone (Android or iOS). Deposit TRON or TRX from any exchange in TRON LINK PRO. Now copy referral link and paste in Dapp brower and click on the arrow on the right corner of the search box area. It will open TRONLIVE.NET and there enter the investment amount and click on invest button. Remember to keep minimum 8-20 Trx extra to pay network fee to avoid failure or transaction.
If you are using laptop or desktop PC then use Google chrome browser and download TRON LINK PRO wallet and click on add to chrome. Create your wallet name. Save all wallet recovery phrases somewhere in a secured place. Now open the TRONLIVE.NET website, paste the referral link and enter investment amount. Click on invest button, it will automatically open the wallet, click on confirm button to make successful transaction.

Can I withdraw anytime?

Yes you can withdraw anytime. No restrictions for that.

Where my withdrawn amount will be transferred?

It will be sent directly to your TRON LINK PRO wallet.

Can I withdraw my active deposit?

You can only withdraw the amount shown in available withdrawal amount. Active deposit is must for getting daily ROI.

How much is minimum withdrawal limit?

Minimum withdrawal limit is 10 Trx.

How re-investment works?

Re-investment is a beneficial function for users. You can re-invest your total available withdrawal amount by clicking on REINVEST button. The re-investment amount will be added to your active deposit and you will start earning compounding interest on active deposit amount. This way you can achieve your 200% profit faster than the standard ROI format.

What is Insurance?

On every withdrawal 25% amount is deducted and is accumulated in an INSURANCE POOL FUND. This fund is for safety of investors. If for any reason or system glitch if smart contract is unable to pay returns of investors then insurance fund will be used to compensate investor’s funds. So there is no chance of losing money in TRONLIVE platform.

Can I withdraw Insurance Pool Fund?

No. No one can withdraw Insurance Pool Fund as this is a safety fund kept for future to secure investor’s funds.

Can I get commission if my downline re-invest?

Yes. You can get commission on every re-invest of your downline. This is the beauty of this platform. And that is why you should promote to more n more people.

How much is minimum re-investment amount?

Minimum re-investment amount is 10 Trx.
